How To Play

  • Register on the site with appropriate credentials. Your provided phone number will be the username used to sign in.

  • Market is open from 9am to 3:30pm on 19th and 20th August 2016.

  • Each user gets Rs.10000 to start with.

  • Initial stock buy ins when the game starts would be directly from the companies under the `Buy Shares` option.

  • To sell your shares, put up an offer under the `Sell Shares` option. Similarly, to buy shares, search for offers of buy directly from the company (if applicable|@ current price) under the `Buy Shares` options.

  • Your transaction history can be seen for each company separately.

  • The leaderboard is public. The rank is determined by a user's networth ie- Total user cash + Total user share value at current price

  • Any attempts at using fake users to climb up ranks will lead to disqualification.

  • Decision of the organizers are final and binding.
